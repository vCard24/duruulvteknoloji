World-Class Manufacturing

Founded in 2003, Yiğit Çelik Kapı operates from a modern 10,000 m² facility in Kayseri 1st Organised Industrial Zone. Steady growth since establishment has placed us among respected companies in the sector, and our innovative approach and strong production capacity have secured a leading position.

With an annual production capacity of 40,000 doors, Yiğit Çelik Kapı exports to Europe, the Middle East and Africa, demonstrating quality and security standards at international level.

Under the registered “YÇK Yiğit Çelik Kapı” brand, we combine security and aesthetics; with TSE fire-resistance certificates and production aligned to international standards, we hold a trusted position in the industry.
