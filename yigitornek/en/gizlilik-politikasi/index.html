Privacy Policy

Protection of your personal data and privacy

At Yiğit Çelik Kapı, protecting the privacy and security of your personal data is one of our highest priorities. This privacy policy explains how your personal data is collected, processed and protected under Turkish Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data (KVKK).

Important: Our website contains only an order form and a contact form. By completing these forms, the user is deemed to have accepted the privacy policy.

01

Introduction

Yiğit Çelik Kapı ("the Company") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of personal data obtained through the website https://yigitcelikkapi.com.tr/.

This privacy policy explains the principles governing the processing of personal data under Turkish Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data ("KVKK").

The security of your personal data is a priority for us. All your data is collected over encrypted connections and stored on secure servers.

02

Data Collection and Purpose of Use

Our website contains only an order form and a contact form. When forms are submitted, the information is sent directly to the company's Hotmail email address.

Information Collected
Name, surname, telephone number, email address, customer note and order information
Purpose of Use
This information is used solely to contact the customer and manage the order process

Personal data collected is processed for the following purposes:

Customer Communication

Contacting the customer

Order Process

Managing the order process

Price Quotes

Evaluating quote requests

Legal Obligations

Fulfilling legal obligations

03

Data Retention Period

Data is retained until the customer requests deletion. Upon the customer's request, the data is deleted without delay.

Important: Statutory retention periods for legal obligations (such as invoice records) also apply separately. Under the Turkish Tax Procedure Law and Turkish Commercial Code, commercial records and documents must be retained for 10 years.

04

Cross-Border Data Transfers

Information submitted through user forms is processed via Microsoft's Hotmail email service.

Because Hotmail servers may be located outside Türkiye, cross-border transfer of data may occur. This is stated explicitly and, where required, the user's explicit consent is obtained.

Under Article 9 of KVKK, the explicit consent of the data subject is obtained for cross-border data transfers, or one of the other conditions set out in KVKK is sought. Microsoft complies with international data protection standards.

05

Cookies

No cookies are collected on our website for any purpose. Users' personal data is not processed through cookies.

If cookies are used in the future, a cookie management panel will be provided to users with the ability to customise preferences.
