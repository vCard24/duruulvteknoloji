Quality policy

Our approach to continuous improvement and excellence aims to meet customer expectations.

Our Quality Commitment

Quality is not a destination but an ongoing process of continuous improvement. With responsibility toward our employees, the environment and society, our quality policy below aims to fully meet customer expectations.

01

Safe, Quality Products

Supply safe, effective materials to quality standards; serve with quality-approved products.

02

Customer Satisfaction

Target customer satisfaction; ensure and increase satisfaction through product quality, technical support and after-sales service.

03

Employee Participation

Ensure all employees participate in our continuous improvement through the conscious involvement of motivated, ambitious staff.

04

Cooperation and Trust

Work with partners and suppliers in mutual cooperation and trust to increase efficiency.

05

Continuous Improvement

Continuously improve all processes affecting our company's success and product quality.

06

Training and Development

Increase employees' individual contributions and skills through ongoing training.

07

Industry Leadership

Remain a leading organisation in our sectors through quality and service excellence.

08

Standards and Compliance

Continuously improve the Quality Management System, maintain standard requirements and ensure full compliance with official and legal requirements.

Our Quality Commitment

At Yiğit Çelik Kapı, we pursue excellence in all processes under our quality policy and commit to delivering products and services at the highest standards. Quality is a continuous journey for us.
