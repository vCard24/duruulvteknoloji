Personal Data Protection Notice

Information under Turkish Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data

In accordance with Turkish Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data (KVKK — Turkey's personal data protection legislation), you can find our notice regarding the processing of your personal data below.

01

Introduction

This privacy notice has been prepared by Yiğit Çelik Kapı ("DATA CONTROLLER") pursuant to Article 10 of Turkish Law No. 6698 on the Protection of Personal Data ("THE LAW"), to inform data subjects about the processing of personal data collected through the website https://yigitcelikkapi.com.tr/.

The DATA CONTROLLER exercises the utmost care to ensure that personal data is processed lawfully, protected and kept secure.

02

Data Collection and Purpose of Use

Our website contains only an order form and a contact form. By completing these forms, the user is deemed to have accepted the privacy policy.

When forms are submitted, the information is sent directly to the company's Hotmail email address.

Information Collected

Name, surname, telephone number, email address, customer note and order information

Purpose of Use

This information is used solely to contact the customer and manage the order process

Your personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Contacting the customer
  • Managing the order process
  • Evaluating and responding to quote requests
  • Evaluating requests and complaints
  • Fulfilling legal obligations
03

Data Retention Period

Your personal data is retained until the customer requests deletion. Upon the customer's request, the data is deleted without delay.

Important: Statutory retention periods for legal obligations (such as invoice records) also apply separately. Under the Turkish Tax Procedure Law and Turkish Commercial Code, commercial records and documents must be retained for 10 years.

04

Cookies

No cookies are collected on our website for any purpose. Users' personal data is not processed through cookies.

If cookies are used in the future, a cookie management panel will be provided to users with the ability to customise preferences.

05

Cross-Border Data Transfers

Information submitted through user forms is processed via Microsoft's Hotmail email service.

Because Hotmail servers may be located outside Türkiye, cross-border transfer of data may occur. This is stated explicitly and, where required, the user's explicit consent is obtained.

Under Article 9 of KVKK, the explicit consent of the data subject is obtained for cross-border data transfers, or one of the other conditions set out in KVKK is sought. Microsoft complies with international data protection standards.

06

Data Subject Rights and Applications

Under Article 11 of KVKK, as data subjects you have the following rights:

To learn whether your personal data is being processed

To request deletion, correction or cessation of processing of your data

To learn the purpose of processing and whether data is used in accordance with that purpose

To know the third parties to whom data has been transferred domestically or abroad

To request compensation for damage suffered as a result of unlawful processing

How to apply: For these requests you may use the contact form or the company email address (info@yigitcelikkapi.com.tr).
